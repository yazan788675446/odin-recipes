Chicken-Mushroom

General Information:

A classic European dish featuring tender chicken breasts smothered in a rich, creamy mushroom sauce. Its elegant, delicious, and pair perfectly with mashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts (thinly sliced)
  • 200g fresh mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Salt, black pepper, and dried oregano

Preparation Steps:

  1. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and oregano.
  2. Sear chicken in butter over medium-high heat until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© garlic and mushrooms until softened.
  4. Add heavy cream and simmer until the sauce thickens
  5. Return chicken to the pan, coat with sauce, and serve hot.
