General Information:

Lasagna is a world-famous Italian dish made of stacked layers of flat pasta alternated with fillings such as ground beef, tomato sauce, and plenty of cheese. It is a hearty, comforting meal baked to perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 12 Lasagna noodles (boiled or oven-ready)
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can (400g) tomato sauce
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 cup Ricotta cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt, pepper, and dried basil

Preparation Steps

  1. In a large pan, brown the ground beef with onion and garlic until fully cooked.
  2. Stir in the tomato sauce and dried basil. Let it simmer for 10 minutes
  3. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom
  4. Layer with lasagna noodles, followed by Ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and Mozzarella
  5. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a thick layer of Mozzarella on top.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 190Â°C for about 30 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
